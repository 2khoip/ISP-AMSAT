Hardware/Materials
------------------

To create and interface with an antenna, you will need:

* 1x long shaft or pole, such as a 2x4 or pipe

* 8x smaller shafts/poles, preferably not metallic/conductive

* 8x 12-24" piano wire

* Coaxial cable (20 ohm)

* Soldering Iron/Toolkit

* RTL-SDR
